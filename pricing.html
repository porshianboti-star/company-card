Pricing FAQ

Is CompanyCard really free?

Yes. The free plan gives you a full digital business card — QR code, sharing link and unlimited updates — with no credit card required. You only pay if you want Pro or team features.

What do the paid plans add?

Paid plans add things like custom branding, Apple & Google Wallet passes, lead capture, view-and-save analytics, and — on team plans — centrally managed cards for every employee.

Do I need a credit card to start?

No. You can create and share a working card on the free plan without entering any payment details.

Can I cancel or change plans anytime?

Yes. Plans are flexible — you can upgrade, downgrade or cancel whenever you like, and your card keeps working on the free plan.

Is there a plan for teams?

Yes. CompanyCard for Teams bills per employee and adds shared branding, roles and analytics across the whole company. See the teams page or talk to sales.

What is CompanyCard?

CompanyCard is a digital business card for professionals and small-to-medium businesses. You share your details in one tap by QR, link, wallet pass or email signature — and the person receiving your card never needs to install an app.
