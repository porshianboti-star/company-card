Frequently asked questions

How do I add a virtual background in Zoom?

Zoom → Settings → Background & effects → add image, and upload the background you generated. Google Meet and Microsoft Teams have the same option in their video settings.

What size should a virtual background be?

1920×1080 (16:9). CompanyCard generates your background at exactly this size so it looks sharp on every platform.

Can people really scan a QR from a video call?

Yes — modern phone cameras read QR codes off screens easily. Keep the code in a corner at the generated size and it scans from a typical laptop video tile.

Can my whole team get matching backgrounds?

Yes. On team plans every employee's background is generated from the same locked template — names change, brand doesn't.
