Why teams switch together

Individually adopted cards create a new mess: fourteen employees, nine designs, three misspelled company names. Digital business cards for teams solve the actual corporate problem — brand consistency at scale — by generating every card from one template that only admins can change.

The complements matter more at team scale. Every CompanyCard seat includes a matching email signature and virtual background, which means every email your company sends and every video call it takes carries the same brand. That's thousands of impressions a month you're currently leaving unbranded.

Offboarding is instant too: remove an employee and their card is gone — no ex-employees handing out live company cards. See the full team story on our For Teams page or compare plans on pricing.

Frequently asked questions

How do employees get their cards?

Admins send an invite link (or import from CSV / Google Workspace / Microsoft 365). Each employee creates their own login and receives a card generated from the company template.

Can employees see each other's cards?

No. Employees see and edit only their own card. Admins see every card in the company. Different companies are completely isolated from each other.

What happens when someone leaves?

Remove them in the dashboard — their card, signature and access are revoked immediately.

Do all employees get the email signature and virtual background?

Yes, both are generated per employee from the same brand template on team plans.
