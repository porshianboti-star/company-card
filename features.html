Features FAQ

What can a CompanyCard digital business card do?

One card holds everything: your contact details, links and socials, shareable by QR code, link, Apple/Google Wallet pass or email signature. It updates live, captures leads, shows analytics, and even works as a branded video-call background.

Does the person receiving my card need an app?

No. Your card opens in any browser with a one-tap "Save contact" button. Only you need an account — the people you share with never install anything.

Does it work with Apple Wallet and Google Wallet?

Yes. You can add your CompanyCard to Apple Wallet and Google Wallet so it's always one tap away on your phone.

Can I use my card as a video-call background?

Yes. CompanyCard generates a branded virtual background with your name and QR code for Zoom, Google Meet and Microsoft Teams, so anyone on the call can scan and save your details.

Can I see who viewed and saved my card?

Yes. Live analytics show views and saves over time, so you know which events, links and placements actually generate contacts.

What is CompanyCard?

CompanyCard is a digital business card for professionals and small-to-medium businesses — a single, always-current card you share in one tap, with no app required for the recipient.
