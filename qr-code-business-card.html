Why QR codes fixed the business card

The QR code solved the last problem of networking: transferring details accurately in the moment. No spelling emails out loud, no "I'll text you". One scan, and your name, number, email, company and links are on their screen with a save button.

The trick is what the QR points to. A static QR that only opens a website wastes the moment. A QR code business card opens a profile designed for one action — saving you as a contact — and it keeps working forever, because the destination updates when your details change.

With CompanyCard, your QR comes attached to a full digital business card, wallet passes for offline sharing, and analytics that show you when and how often your code is scanned.
