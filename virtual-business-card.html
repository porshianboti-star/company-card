Virtual business card vs. paper: the honest comparison

The average paper business card is thrown away within a week — and the details on it start expiring the day it's printed. A virtual business card flips that: it costs nothing to "reprint", it can't be lost, and it gets smarter over time as you add links, offers and content.

Virtual cards also close the loop. When someone opens your card, you can see it. When they save your contact or leave their details through lead capture, that's a warm follow-up you would never get from paper.

It's a business card that lives online at a personal link, instead of on paper. It holds your contact details, photo and links, and can be shared by QR code, link, wallet pass or email signature.

Is a virtual business card professional?
