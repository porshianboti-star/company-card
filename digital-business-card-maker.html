From card makers to card platforms

Classic business card makers end at the print button — you design, you order 500 units, and the box is obsolete by the time your role changes. A digital business card maker ends at a live link instead: same design pleasure, zero print cost, and a card that stays correct forever.

It also changes what a "card" can contain. In the CompanyCard maker you can add a booking calendar, video, payment links and social profiles — and reorder everything with drag and drop. Try holding a Calendly link on cardstock.

If you still like paper's ritual, print a minimal card with just your name and your QR code — and let the digital card carry everything that changes. Start with the free plan.

Frequently asked questions

Is the digital business card maker free?

Yes — designing and publishing your card, including the QR code and link, is part of the free plan. Premium templates and custom branding come with Pro.

Can I match my company's brand?

Yes. Set brand colours, logo and cover in the maker. On team plans, admins lock the template so every employee's card stays on brand.

Can I make cards for my whole team?

Yes — the team onboarding flow imports employees from CSV, Google Workspace or Microsoft 365 and issues everyone a card from one locked template.

Do I need design skills?

No. Templates handle the design; you only fill in your details and pick colours with a live preview.
