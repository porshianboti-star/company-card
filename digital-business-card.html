What is a digital business card?

A digital business card is an online profile that replaces the traditional paper card. Instead of handing someone a piece of card they will probably lose, you share a link or let them scan a QR code — and your full contact details, photo, social profiles and booking links land straight in their phone.

Unlike paper, a digital business card is dynamic. Promoted? Changed your number? Edit once and everyone who ever received your card sees the new version. It also works both ways: with lead capture enabled, people you meet can leave their details with you, so a handshake becomes pipeline instead of a forgotten stack of cards.

CompanyCard cards work on any device with no app required for the receiver, include an email signature and video-call background for every user, and scale from one professional to a whole company with team management.

Frequently asked questions

Is a digital business card free?

Yes. CompanyCard's free plan includes a full digital business card with QR code, sharing link and unlimited updates. Paid plans add custom branding, lead capture, analytics and team management.

Does the other person need an app?

No. Your card opens in any web browser. The receiver scans your QR code or taps your link and can save your contact in one tap.

Can I update my card after sharing it?

Yes — that's the point. Your card is a live page: edit your details once and every QR code and link you've already shared shows the new version instantly.

How do people save my details?

Your card includes a 'Save contact' button that downloads a vCard (.vcf) straight into their phone's contacts — with your photo, title, phone, email and links.
