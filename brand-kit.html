Brand Guidelines · v1.0

CompanyCard Brand Kit

The essentials for keeping CompanyCard looking and sounding like itself — logo, colour, type, slogan, voice and messaging. www.company-card.com

Name

CompanyCard

One word, camel-case. Never "Company Card" or "company card".

Domain

company-card.com

Always lower-case. Pair with https://www. in formal use.

Mission

Replace paper, keep people

Make every introduction instant, beautiful and measurable — for individuals and teams.

Identity

Logo

The mark pairs a contactless "C" — three radiating arcs that read as both the letter C and a tap-to-share signal — set on a rounded card in the brand gradient. Give it room to breathe: keep clear space of at least the height of the icon on all sides.

Primary lockup — on light backgrounds
Reversed — on dark / photo backgrounds
Icon — app, favicon & avatar
Palette

Colour

Indigo-to-violet is the heart of the brand. Use the gradient for hero moments and the icon; use solid indigo for primary actions; keep large surfaces neutral and let colour lead the eye.

Voice

Slogan & taglines

The primary slogan leads with the product's magic — one gesture, a lasting relationship. Supporting taglines flex for different contexts.

One tap. Every connection.
The digital business card for people & teams. Share who you are, instantly. Your network, in one tap. Make every handshake count.
Voice & tone

How we sound

Confident but warm. Modern but human. We're the easy, obvious upgrade to the paper card — so we speak plainly, lead with benefits, and never overcomplicate.

Do say

  • "Share your card in one tap."
  • "Always up to date — change it once."
  • "Built for you, and your whole team."
  • "No app to download."

Don't say

  • "Leverage our synergistic contact-exchange paradigm."
  • "Revolutionary disruptive solution."
  • "Digitalize your interpersonal touchpoints."
  • Jargon, hype, or buzzwords.
Messaging

Five things we always say

Every page, ad and pitch ladders back to these pillars.

Instant

Share in one tap — QR, link, wallet or email signature. No app required.

Always current

Update once and every card you've shared updates everywhere.

Measurable

Capture contacts and see views, taps and saves in real time.

On-brand at scale

One consistent identity across every employee and card.

Sustainable

Zero paper waste — the card that never ends up in the bin.
